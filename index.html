A Poem of Love


                Your smile is the light of my day,
                Chasing all the shadows away.
                With you, I have found my home,
                No matter how far I may roam.

                Your voice is a calming song,
                That keeps my heart steady and strong.
                In your arms, I feel safe and free,
                You are the only one for me.

                I love you more than words can say,
                In every single, quiet way.
                Together, our future is bright,
                My love, my joy, my shining light.
